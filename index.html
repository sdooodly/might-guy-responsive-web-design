Might Guy Fan Club

Folks, welcome to the Might Guy Fan Club, where the enthusiasm for our green spandex-clad hero is through the roof! Our club is dedicated to celebrating everything about Guy - from his ridiculous bowl haircut to his fiery passion for youthfulness.

About Us

We're not just any ordinary fan club, oh no. We don't just scream and cheer for Guy's every move. We take things to the next level! We do squats in his honor, eat only vegetables to channel his inner strength, and run laps around the block while shouting "dynamic entry!" at every turn. Youthfulness awaits!
